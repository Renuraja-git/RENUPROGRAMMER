We know that you guys are eagerly waiting for us to get to know today special.Today we are going to prepare Chicken Briyani

Ingredients:

  1. Cooked Rice
  2. Chicken
  3. Onion
  4. Tomato
  5. Garam Masala
  6. Giger Garlic paste
  7. Pudhina leaves
  8. Salt
  9. Turmeric Powder
  10. Chilly Powder
  11. Coriander leaves

Preparation:

  • Add oil to a pot or pressure cooker and heat it.
  • Add onions and fry till they turn golden.
  • Next add chicken and fry till the color slightly changes to pale
  • Add Ginger Garlic paste.
  • Add Garam masala.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, chili powder and mint.
  • Cook till the mixture turns thick and dry
