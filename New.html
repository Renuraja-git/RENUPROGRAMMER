We know that you guys are eagerly waiting for us to get to know today special.Today we are going to prepare Chicken Briyani

Ingredients:

  1. Cooked Rice
  2. Chicken
  3. Onion
  4. Tomato
  5. Garam Masala
  6. Giger Garlic paste
  7. Pudhina leaves
  8. Salt
  9. Turmeric Powder
  10. Chilly Powder
  11. Coriander leaves

Steps to preparation

  1. Add oil to a pot or pressure cooker and heat it.

  2. Add onions and fry till they turn golden.

  3. Next add chicken and fry till the color slightly changes to pale.

  4. Add Ginger Garlic paste.

  5. Add Garam masala.

  6. Add chopped tomatoes, chili powder and mint.

  7. Cook till the mixture turns thick and dry. Add boiled rice and Once the biryani is cooked, let it rest for 5-10 minutes, and finish by scattering the remaining onions on top. Serve hot digging the spoon deep to get all the layers.
